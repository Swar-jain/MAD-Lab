What we do?

Lumiere is a multidisciplinary creative space. We are based in Chennai. We work together to design, create and produce work that we are proud of for people and organizations that we believe in. We are available for hire in a wide range of creative disciplines for a variety of jobs, projects and gigs. There are a lot of agencies that do what we do. They share the same what and how, but our partners work with us for our why and our who. We’re minds and makers with business sense and creative chops, set out to connect people with what matters most — the experience. And we spend each day doing so by sharpening the tools of the digital trade.
